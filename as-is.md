Project Name:- Phone accessories website

Overview:- A customer adds an item to their cart and proceeds through checkout via creating their profile, address and the payment entry. Shipping costs is only revealed when they are about to do the payment and estimated date for delivery is not revealed until they have done the payment. Guest checkout exists but is not clearly presented as an option on homepage, therefore many users have to procees towards account creation.


Pain Points:-
Total cost(shipping/taxes/service charges) is not revealed until the final payment step.
Guest checkout is not easily visible on home page, leading some users to visit compeititor's website/store instead.
Delivery date may or may not meet the customer's expectation, until it's revealed.
If the customer is not satisfied with expected delivery date than they might cancel the order or to reach out customer service for additional help.
There is no post-order real-time tracking — customers only get a static confirmation email, then must check back manually for updates.


Stakeholders:-
Customers, Customer service, Engineering and designing team.
