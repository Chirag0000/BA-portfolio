Project:- To improve user experience on payment gateway and post-order experience.

Executive summary:- The current system where final and total cost is not revealed until the user has finished all the steps and post-order experience is challenging for customer, tends to leave a bad image of the company. Eventually, this leads to a loss of order due to complex procedures. This project proposes to address these pain points by making website user friendly, by making total cost visible when user adds the product to the cart. Moreover, working with delivery partners to reuduce the shipping time and improving post-order experience by providing them the link, so they can track their orders. This will eventually, reduce the workload on customer service and improve the financials of the organization.

Business obective:- 
1) Improve the customer satisfaction by providing transparent and seamless system.
2) Reducing the work load on Customer service, so they could actually engage with customers for after service support.
3) Reduce order cancellation caused by unacceptable delivery estimates.
4) Improved adoption via guest checkout.

Scope:-
In Scope:-
1) Displaying total cost at the cart level.
2) Displaying delivery date during the payment gateway
3) Providing guest sign in available at the homepage.
4) Providing post-order real time tracking

Out-Scope:-
1) Changes to product catalog and promotions
2) Changes to warehouse strategies.

Stakeholders:- Customers, Customer service, engineering and design.

Current State:- Customers view the final cost when they are about to make payment and they don't get to know any delivery date untill they have made the payment. Moreover, there is a lack of post-order tracking facility. This whole process gives out a bad reputation for the company and customer service is bombarded with questions.

Future State:- Customers are able to see the estimated costs in cart and delivery date is visibleas well. Post-order tracking is accessible once the payment is made and guest sign up and guest checkout is available.

Assumption:-
1) shipping cost and tax to included when the user adds item to the cart.
2) Real-time tracking will be integrated with the website.
3) Delivery date will be visible with the cost paid by customer.

Constraints:-
1) Changes made to the system by adding the shipping cost and tax at cart system should not slow down the overall system.
2) Guest checkout visibility change should not reduce account creation process.

Success Criteria:-
1) Customer service inbound calls reduced.
2) Order dissatisfaction tied to delivery-date decreases.
3) Guest account sign up increase.
